I am the William Wulf Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science @ UVa, where I lead the NLP group. My research interests include natural language processing and machine learning.

From Aug. 2016 to Jul. 2018, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering @ UW, where I worked with Prof. Noah Smith. I finished my Ph.D. in 2016 from the Computational Linguistics Lab at Georgia Tech. My Ph.D. advisor was Prof. Jacob Eisenstein.
